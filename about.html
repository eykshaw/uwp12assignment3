About This Blog

This blog, written by UC Davis student Emily Shaw, discusses podcasts pertaining to architecture history. The first series, an analysis of the architecture history podcast genre, looks at three podcasts and how they use rhetorical devices to fulfill their rhetorical purpose.

Discussed Podcasts

  • 99% Invisible
